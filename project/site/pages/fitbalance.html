What is FitBalance?

This web app is your smart training partner – built to help you plan workouts, track them live, and calculate your calorie burn in real time. Easily log your water intake and stay hydrated throughout the day. For strength training, explore a visual muscle map to learn about each muscle and find targeted exercises. All your data is stored locally and shown clearly in your personal dashboard – giving you a full overview of your fitness progress at any time.
